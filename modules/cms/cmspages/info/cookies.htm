What cookies do we store?

PHPfy stores session cookie and cookie policy acceptance cookie (to not to show you annoying cookie popup every time you open this website)

Some other cookies coming from external services may be stored. Please check their cookie policy as well.

External service providers:

  • Google - OAuth, tracking and analytics
  • Facebook - OAuth
  • LinkedIn - OAuth
  • GitHub - OAuth

Can I disable cookies?

Yes. But expect some difficulties when using the service.
